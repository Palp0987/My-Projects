Veg Biryani

Veg Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with a mix of vegetables, basmati rice, and a variety of spices. Here's a step-by-step recipe to make a delicious Veg Biryani:

01

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice, soaked in water for 30 minutes and drained
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, beans, peas, potatoes, etc.), chopped
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt (curd)
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/4 cup fried onions (optional, for garnish)
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • A few saffron strands soaked in milk (or use yellow food color)
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter) or vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon biryani masala or garam masala
  • Whole Spices: 1-inch cinnamon stick, 4-5 cloves, 4-5 green cardamoms, 1-2 bay leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Water for boiling the rice
  • Cashews and raisins (optional, for garnish)

02

Instructions:

  1. Heat ghee or oil in a large heavy-bottomed pan or pot over medium heat. Add the whole spices (cinnamon, cloves, cardamoms, bay leaves) and sauté for a minute until they release their aroma.
  2. Add the sliced onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables and cook for a few minutes until they are slightly tender. You can blanch or parboil the vegetables before adding them to the pot if you prefer them softer.
  4. Stir in the chopped mint and coriander leaves, and half of the fried onions (if using). Reserve the other half for garnishing.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix the yogurt with biryani masala or garam masala. Add this mixture to the pot and mix well with the vegetables.
  6. Now, add the soaked and drained basmati rice to the pot and gently mix it with the vegetables and spices.
  7. Pour water into the pot. The ratio of rice to water should be approximately 1:2 (1 cup rice requires 2 cups of water). Adjust the water quantity based on your rice and pot. Add salt to taste.
  8. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid, and let the biryani cook on low heat for about 15-20 minutes. Alternatively, you can also cook the rice and vegetables separately and then layer them in a dum biryani style.

03

Serving:

Serve the delicious Veg Biryani with raita (yogurt-based side dish), salad, or any curry of your choice. Enjoy the flavorful and aromatic vegetarian delight with your loved ones!
